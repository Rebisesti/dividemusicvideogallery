Drag Me Under - Izuku Midoriya

The main protagonist of the hit anime My Hero Academia, Divide Music covers Izuku during his solo outing in the Vigilante Deku arc. This arc covers the protagonists at his lowest after the main antagonist, Tomura Shigaraki, narrowly won during their previous fight which led to Izuku going into the hospital as he raised hell at several supermax prisons and freed his master, All For One. This chaos causes his home country to fall into chaos and hope in heroes has shattered. Separating himself from his friends to have Shigaraki focus his attention on him, he goes hunting for escaped convicts and would be assassins sent by All For One. His mentality and hope dwindle over the arc as he tries to keep himself together on the brink of starvation and collapse.

Who's Standing Now? - Dragon Ball

Whilst it is inspired by the Dragon Ball Anime this song was inspired by the video game Dragon Ball Budakai Tenkaichi 4, which just came out at the time this song was released. The song goes over the numerous heroes within the Dragon Ball story, primarily focussing on the Z-Fighters.

Save You - Superman

Superman. This song goes over the many different iterations of the most infamous superhero in fiction. From one off movies, nostalgic early 2000’s TV shows, stellar video games and numerous comics.

Dark Side - Darth Vader

Darth Vader. One of if not the most well known villain in fictional history. This song covers what everyone loves about the fallen jedi. His power, his design, his story and, of course, his legacy as the dark lord.

Mayday - CUSTOM

Unlike the other songs in this list, MAYDAY is a custom song of Divide's own making. From his album Singularity, I may not know what it's based on, if anything, but it's one of his best songs in his library.
